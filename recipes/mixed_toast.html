French mixed toast

A simple mixed toast made with french ingredients, great for your mourning breakfast!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of french bread
  • 2 slices of brie
  • 2 slices of ham

Steps:

  1. Put the 2 slices of brie in between the slices of ham, in between the two slices of bread
  2. Put the sandwich in the sandwich maker for 2 minutes
  3. Take it from the sandwich maker and cut it in 2 triangles
